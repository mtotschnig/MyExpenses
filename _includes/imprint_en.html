Privacy policy

My Expenses does not access any data on your device other than its own database, its own folder on the SD card. If you use the feature of plans for future and recurrent transactions, you assign the calendar,where these plans are stored. My Expenses does not send any data over the Internet. My Expenses uses the following permissions:

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
This is needed for storing the exported QIF file on the SD card, and for creating database backups.
READ_CALENDAR, WRITE_CALENDAR
Storing plans for future and recurring transactions
